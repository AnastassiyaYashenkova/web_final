Help Center

Why is the website displaying differently on my browser?

IMDb is compatible with most common operating systems and modern web browsers, such as: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Opera, Edge. Some other browsers might not be supported.

I never received my registration email. How do I login?

Check that you entered a valid email and check spam filters. Our automated system sends a one-time password (OTP) verification email.

I forgot my password. How do I login?

Go to the forgotten password page and enter your registered email address to get a reset link.
